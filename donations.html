üìñ About Ecoexist Trust

Ecoexist Trust is dedicated to promoting coexistence between humans and wildlife in Botswana. Through GPS tracking, community engagement, and research, we work to reduce human-elephant conflict while protecting these magnificent animals for future generations.

Your donations directly support our field operations, including elephant collaring, aerial surveys, data analysis, and community outreach programs. 100% of your donation goes directly to conservation work.

Tax Information: Ecoexist Trust is a registered non-profit organization. Donations may be tax-deductible. Please consult your tax advisor.
